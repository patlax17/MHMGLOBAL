Our Training Programs

MHM Global delivers evidence-based mental health training designed for workplaces, educational institutions, and community organizations. All programs are led by certified instructors with clinical expertise.

safeTALK

4 Hours Certification

Suicide Alertness for Everyone

Participants learn to recognize signs of suicide risk and connect individuals to appropriate resources. This gatekeeper training builds confidence in having direct conversations about suicide.

  • Identify persons with thoughts of suicide
  • Apply the TALK steps (Tell, Ask, Listen, KeepSafe)
  • Connect individuals to intervention resources
  • Receive certification upon completion

ASIST

2 Days Certification

Applied Suicide Intervention Skills Training

Comprehensive two-day workshop teaching suicide first aid. Participants learn to provide immediate intervention and develop safety plans with individuals at risk.

  • Conduct suicide safety assessments
  • Develop collaborative safety plans
  • Apply intervention models in real scenarios
  • International certification recognized globally

QPR

2 Hours Referral Skills

Question, Persuade, Refer

Efficient gatekeeper training teaching the three steps anyone can use to help prevent suicide. Ideal for large-scale workforce implementation.

  • Question individuals about suicide
  • Persuade them to seek help
  • Refer to appropriate resources
  • Certificate of completion provided

Workplace Equity

90 Minutes HR Focus

Mental Health Stigma Reduction for Leaders

Designed for HR professionals and organizational leaders. Addresses mental health stigma, accommodation strategies, and building psychologically safe workplaces.

  • Understand mental health in workplace context
  • Implement reasonable accommodations
  • Create stigma-free culture
  • Develop mental health policies
