Become an MHM Envoy

Join our network of trained community leaders bringing mental health support to underserved populations.

What is an MHM Envoy?

MHM Envoys are trained volunteers who serve as mental health champions in their communities. Through comprehensive training and ongoing support, Envoys facilitate healing circles, provide peer support, and connect community members to resources.

Envoy Roles

Healing Circle Facilitator

Lead peer support groups in your community.

SOS Responder

Provide de-escalation support during crises.

Community Educator

Deliver mental health awareness workshops.

Resource Navigator

Connect individuals to clinical services.

Training & Support

  • 40-hour comprehensive training program
  • Certification in trauma-informed care
  • Ongoing supervision and peer support
  • Access to clinical consultation
  • Professional development opportunities
