Our Key Initiatives
01
Global Reach

The Global Mental Health Initiative

Promotes mental health wellness for vulnerable and underserved communities globally, advancing awareness and access through targeted wellness missions around the world.

Learn More
02
Community

The Community Mental Health Initiative

Hosts curated community conversations designed to de-stigmatize and normalize mental health, educate, and provide screenings, trainings, and other wellness resources.

Learn More
03
Violence Prevention

The SOS Project Initiative

Works collaboratively with community violence prevention programs to provide resources, trainings, and summits that highlight the impact of interpersonal gun violence and offer healing through trauma recovery practices.

Learn More
04
Education & Training

The MHM Global Training Institute

Instructs and facilitates various mental health training courses and workshops, equipping individuals, organizations, and community leaders with the tools they need to promote wellness.

Learn More
05
Arts & Culture

The Creative Arts Initiative

Raises awareness and educates on mental health topics through film, visual arts, narrative storytelling, and music. Also provides programming that explores healing through the arts as a therapeutic pathway.

Learn More
06
Restorative Justice

Healing Justice Initiative

Provides spaces of healing and support for individuals impacted by the criminal-legal system through Restorative Justice Practices and trainings, fostering accountability, repair, and community wholeness.

Learn More
